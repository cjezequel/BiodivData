# Projects

I'm a GIS Research Engineer at the IRD (Institut de Recherche pour le Développement).


## Main Project — AmazonFISH

My main focus is the study of the distribution of freshwater biodiversity in the Amazon Basin (https://www.amazon-fish.com/) :

- Compilation and Management of a database of freshwater fish species distribution for the entire basin 
(AmazonFISH, transnational collaborative project with 16 institutions in 5 countries, Brazil, Bolivia, Colombia, Peru and Ecuador).
- Expertise in Ecology and Biodiversity Conservation using GIS (biodiversity hotspots, climate change, dam and deforestation effect).
